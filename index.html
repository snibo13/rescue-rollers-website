RESCUE Rollers

Rapid Emergency Search in Cluttered and Unstructured Environments

Overview

Rapid Emergency Search in Cluttered and Unstructured Environments (RESCUE) rollers are an open source platform designed for experimentation in search and rescue. For more details see publications. This is a low-cost and versatile platform that can be used for a variety of applications, including search and rescue, disaster response, and exploration in unstructured environments.
The platform is designed to be modular and extensible, allowing researchers and practitioners to easily adapt it to their specific needs.
